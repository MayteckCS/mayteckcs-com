Starter Website Package

Investment: $1,000 (One-Time Design Fee)

Who is This For?

The Starter Website Package is meticulously crafted for solo owners, ambitious startups, and local businesses taking their first confident step online. If you need a professional, impactful online presence that clearly communicates who you are, what you do, and how to reach you, without the complexity of multiple pages, this package is your perfect launchpad. It’s designed to get you online quickly, efficiently, and affordably, making a strong first impression.

What's Included (Your Project Checklist):

This section details everything you'll receive with your Starter Website Package.

Discovery & Planning:

  • Initial consultation to understand your business goals and audience.
  • Discussion of your brand identity, colors, fonts, and existing marketing materials.
  • Content guidance and recommendations for your single-page structure.

Professional Design & Development:

  • 1-Page Site with Scrollable Sections: A beautifully designed, cohesive single-page website that intelligently organizes your key information into distinct, easy-to-navigate sections (e.g., About, Services, Contact).
    • Common sections include: Hero/Introduction, About Us/Our Story, Services/Offerings (summary), Contact Information (with simple form), Testimonials (optional), Call to Action.
  • Mobile-Optimized & Fast-Loading Design: Your website will look stunning and function flawlessly on all devices – desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. We prioritize speed to ensure an excellent user experience and better search engine performance.
  • Clean Layout with Client Branding: A modern, intuitive layout tailored to your brand's aesthetic. We integrate your logo, brand colors, and typography to ensure a consistent and professional online image.
  • Essential User Interface Elements:
    • Clear navigation for scrollable sections.
    • Prominent call-to-action buttons.

Basic Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Foundations:

  • Meta Title & Description Setup: We'll craft compelling and keyword-rich meta titles and descriptions for your single page to help search engines understand your site's content and encourage clicks from search results.
  • Image Optimization: Basic optimization of images for faster loading times and better search engine visibility.

Website Launch & Handoff:

  • Final review and approval process.
  • Deployment of your website to your chosen hosting platform.
  • Confirmation of site functionality and mobile responsiveness.
  • All sites are fully owned by the client.
  • All sites are built to convert visitors into customers.
  • All sites are custom-fit to your business goals.

Our Process (Your Step-by-Step Journey):

We believe in a collaborative and transparent process. Here's how we'll bring your Starter Website to life:

1. Discovery Call & Quote:

We start with a free consultation to discuss your vision, business, and needs. Based on this, we provide a detailed proposal and quote.

2. Contract & Deposit:

Once you approve the proposal, we sign a simple agreement, and a 50% deposit secures your project in our schedule.

3. Content Collection:

We'll provide a clear content guide and work with you to gather all necessary text, images, and brand assets for your site.

4. Design & Initial Build:

Based on our discussions, we'll design and build the first draft of your one-page website.

5. Review & Revisions (1 Round):

You'll receive a private link to review your site. We'll then collaborate on one round of revisions to fine-tune the design and content based on your feedback.

6. Final Approval & Balance Payment:

Once you're thrilled with your website, you'll give us final approval, and the remaining 50% balance will be due.

7. Launch & Handover:

We'll launch your website, ensuring everything is perfect. We'll provide you with login details and basic guidance for ownership.

Monthly Services (Crucial for Client Success):

A website isn't a "set it and forget it" asset. To ensure your site remains secure, fast, and visible to your ideal customers, we offer these essential ongoing monthly services.

1. Website Hosting & Technical Support

Investment: Starting at $10/month (price varies based on traffic needs and site complexity)

What's Included:

  • Secure & Reliable Hosting: Your website lives on high-performance servers, ensuring maximum uptime and fast loading speeds for your visitors.
  • SSL Certificate: An essential security layer (https://) encrypting data between your site and visitors, crucial for trust and SEO.
  • Daily Backups: Regular automated backups of your entire website, so you never have to worry about data loss.
  • Core Software Updates: We manage updates to your website's core platform (e.g., WordPress, Squarespace, godaddy) to ensure security and compatibility.
  • Security Monitoring: Proactive monitoring to detect and prevent potential threats or vulnerabilities.
  • Technical Support: Access to our team for any technical issues related to your website's functionality or hosting.
  • (Note: This does not include content updates or new feature development, though we are always happy to discuss upgrades with you!)

2. Monthly SEO (Search Engine Optimization) Service

Investment: Averages at $150/month (Customized plans available based on goals)

Why Monthly SEO is Crucial:

While your Starter Website has basic SEO setup, ongoing SEO is what truly drives organic traffic, helps you rank higher, and connects you with customers actively searching for your services. Think of it as your ongoing marketing engine.

What's Included (Basic Monthly SEO):

  • Keyword Research & Monitoring: Ongoing research to identify new opportunities and track your current keyword performance.
  • Content Optimization (for your existing page): Suggestions and light revisions to your page's content to improve keyword density and readability.
  • Technical SEO Audit (Quarterly): Regular checks for site speed, mobile-friendliness, and other technical factors impacting SEO.
  • Google My Business Optimization: For local businesses, optimizing and managing your Google My Business profile is key to local search visibility.
  • Basic Analytics Reporting: Monthly reports on your website's traffic, search performance, and key metrics.
  • Competitor Analysis (Basic): Understanding what your local competitors are doing in search.
  • Strategy Call (Monthly/Bi-Monthly): A dedicated call to discuss performance, new opportunities, and future SEO strategies.
