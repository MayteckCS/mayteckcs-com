Standard Business Site Package

Investment: $1,500 – $2,500 (One-Time Design Fee)

Who is This For?

The Standard Business Site Package is ideal for growing businesses that require a more expansive and stylized online presence than a single-page site can offer. If you're looking to establish a robust digital footprint, showcase multiple services or products, engage visitors with a blog, and provide more comprehensive information, this package is designed for you. It's perfect for businesses ready to expand their online reach and brand identity.

Professional Design & Development:

  • Up to 4 Custom-Designed Pages: Beyond the single page, this package includes the creation of up to four distinct, custom-designed pages.

Our Process (Your Step-by-Step Journey):

We maintain a collaborative and transparent approach throughout your project:

1. Discovery Call & Quote:

Free initial consultation to understand your business, expanded website needs, and goals. We'll provide a detailed proposal and transparent quote.

2. Contract & Deposit:

A signed agreement and 50% deposit secure your project and begin the development process.

3. Content Collection & Planning:

We'll provide comprehensive content guides for each page and work closely with you to gather all necessary text, images, and brand assets.

4. Design & Initial Build:

We design and build the first draft of your multi-page website, focusing on user experience and visual appeal.

5. Review & Revisions (2 Rounds):

You'll receive a private link to review the full site. We'll collaborate through two rounds of revisions to perfect the design, content, and functionality.

6. Final Approval & Balance Payment:

Once you're completely satisfied, you'll give us final approval, and the remaining 50% balance will be due.

7. Launch & Handover:

We launch your fully functional website. We provide you with all necessary login details and basic guidance for managing your site.
