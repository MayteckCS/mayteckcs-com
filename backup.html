3-2-1 Backup Strategy

Keep 3 copies of your data — 2 onsite (e.g. PC and NAS) and 1 offsite (e.g. cloud). This protects your data from physical damage, hardware failure, and cyber threats.

Cloud Backup Integration

We configure encrypted cloud backups to services like Backblaze, Wasabi, or AWS. You’ll enjoy peace of mind with daily snapshots and version control.

Local NAS Setup & Support

On-premise backups using Synology or QNAP systems give fast, local restore access. We manage setup, permissions, and monitoring.

Automated Backup Scheduling

Daily, weekly, or real-time backups — whatever your workflow requires. We build automated tasks to keep your system protected without interruption.

Disaster Recovery Planning

Fire, flood, theft? Our recovery playbooks and offsite strategies ensure your business can resume operations fast with minimal loss.

Real-Time Monitoring & Alerts

We use smart dashboards and monitoring tools to ensure backup jobs complete successfully. If anything fails, we know — and act — before you even notice.
